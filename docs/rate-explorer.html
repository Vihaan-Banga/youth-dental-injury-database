Browse cited dental / orofacial injury rates and mouthguard evidence by sport and age group, drawn straight from the harmonized database.

Read this first. This is a population rate explorer, not an individual risk calculator. The numbers below are population-level surveillance and study estimates. They cannot predict any individual person's risk of a dental injury, and they are not comparable to each other unless they share the same denominator (read each rate together with its denominator). Sources use different definitions, age ranges, settings, and denominators (per 1000 athlete-exposures, per 100,000, per season, % of injuries, …). Treat this as a citation-finding and comparison aid, not clinical advice. Pre-launch data — do not cite externally until v1.0.
